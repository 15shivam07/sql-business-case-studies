# Seasonal Sales Analysis Insights

## Key Findings

* Revenue declined slightly in 2016 before entering a strong growth phase.
* Annual revenue increased from 459K in 2016 to 722K in 2018.
* Q4 (October–December) consistently generated the highest revenue.
* November was the strongest-performing month across the dataset.
* February frequently recorded the lowest sales performance.

## Business Insight

The business demonstrates clear seasonality, with demand increasing significantly during the second half of the year and peaking during Q4.

Recurring year-end peaks suggest strong holiday and seasonal purchasing behavior.

## Business Recommendation

Use seasonal demand patterns to improve inventory planning, marketing campaigns, workforce allocation, and sales forecasting.
