# Product Mix Analysis Insights

## Key Findings

* Technology is the highest revenue-generating category across all regions.
* East records the highest Technology revenue share (39.30%).
* South records the second-highest Technology revenue share (38.08%).
* East and South also record the highest average revenue per order.
* Machine sales are strongest in East and South.

## Business Insight

Regions with stronger Technology sales, particularly premium products such as Machines, tend to generate higher average order values.

The relationship between Technology revenue concentration and customer spending behavior suggests that product mix significantly influences regional performance.

## Business Recommendation

Increase focus on high-value Technology products in lower-performing regions to improve average order value and overall revenue performance.
