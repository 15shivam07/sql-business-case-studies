## Key Insight

West is the largest revenue-generating region and processes the highest number of orders.

East records the highest average revenue per order, indicating stronger customer spending behavior despite lower overall revenue than West.

## Business Recommendation

Revenue should not be evaluated only by total sales. Regions with higher customer spending behavior may present opportunities for premium product strategies and targeted marketing campaigns.
