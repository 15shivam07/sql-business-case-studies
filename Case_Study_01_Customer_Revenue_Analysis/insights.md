# Customer Revenue Analysis Insights

## Key Findings

- Top 10% customers contributed 31.16% of total revenue.
- Top 20% customers contributed 48.00% of total revenue.
- Top 50% customers contributed 80.43% of total revenue.

## Business Insight

A relatively small group of customers generates a significant share of revenue.

## Recommendation

Focus on retaining high-value customers while reducing dependency on a limited customer segment.
