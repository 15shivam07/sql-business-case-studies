# Geographic Revenue Analysis Insights

## Key Findings

* California generated the highest revenue ($446,306.46).
* New York, Texas, Washington, and Pennsylvania were also among the top-performing states.
* The Top 5 states contributed 51.85% of total company revenue.

## Business Insight

Revenue is heavily concentrated within a relatively small number of geographic markets.

California is the dominant revenue-generating state, while a handful of states account for more than half of total company revenue.

## Business Recommendation

Continue investing in high-performing markets while reducing geographic concentration risk through expansion into underperforming states with growth potential.
