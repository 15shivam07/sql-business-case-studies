## Completed Case Studies

### 1. Customer Revenue Concentration Analysis
Business Goal:
Determine how dependent the company is on a small group of customers.

Key Finding:
Top 20% customers generated 48% of total revenue.

Skills Used:
- Aggregations
- Subqueries
- CTEs
- Ranking

---

### 2. Regional Sales Performance Analysis
Business Goal:
Compare revenue, order volume, and customer spending behavior across regions.

Key Finding:
West generated highest revenue while East recorded highest average revenue per order.

Skills Used:
- GROUP BY
- Aggregations
- Business KPI Analysis

---

### 3. Product Mix Analysis
Business Goal:
Identify why some regions achieve higher average order values.

Key Finding:
Technology and Machine sales significantly increased average order value.

Skills Used:
- Category Analysis
- Revenue Contribution Analysis

---

### 4. Seasonal Sales Trend Analysis
Business Goal:
Analyze monthly and yearly sales patterns.

Key Finding:
Q4 consistently generated the highest revenue.

Skills Used:
- Date Functions
- Trend Analysis
- Aggregations

---

### 5. Geographic Revenue Analysis
Business Goal:
Identify geographic concentration risks.

Key Finding:
Top 5 states generated 51.85% of total company revenue.

- Revenue Distribution Analysis
